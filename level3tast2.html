Web Developer Internship Opportunities at Cognifyz Technologies

About the Internship

As an intern, you may assist with coding under the direction of a professional computer programmer, test existing code, format graphics for web use, or communicate with clients about the project. Your responsibilities may also involve testing new technology for potential use in the web development project. Join Cognifyz Technologies for an enriching internship experience. Our program runs for 12 weeks and offers hands-on projects that enhance your skills in web development.

Internship Highlights

  • Gain real-world experience working on live projects.
  • Receive mentorship from experienced developers.
  • Opportunity to collaborate with a dynamic team.
  • Access to the latest development tools and technologies.

Qualifications

  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Familiarity with front-end frameworks like React or Angular.
  • Basic understanding of back-end technologies such as Node.js or Python.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and a willingness to learn.
